Financial Assistance Schemes for Women

Explore financial support and benefit programs aimed at uplifting women’s economic independence.

Subhadra Yojana (Odisha)

Provides ₹10,000 annual aid to women aged 21–60 via direct benefit transfer linked with Aadhaar.

Learn More →

Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana (Maharashtra)

Monthly ₹1,500 payment and additional support for women, encouraging financial stability and welfare.

Learn More →

Palakmantri Makan-Dukan Scheme (Kolhapur)

₹30,000 aid to women for establishing small grocery shops alongside PMAY housing projects.

Learn More →

Mahila Samman Savings Certificate

Special small-savings scheme for women with 7.5% interest rate and easy withdrawal options.
