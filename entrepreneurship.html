Entrepreneurship Schemes for Women

Discover government and NGO programs that support women entrepreneurs in starting and growing their businesses.

Mahila E-Haat

An online platform that enables women entrepreneurs to sell products and services directly to customers.

Stand-Up India Scheme

Provides bank loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 crore to women entrepreneurs for starting new ventures.

PMEGP (Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme)

Offers financial assistance to women for setting up micro-enterprises in manufacturing or service sectors.

Udyogini Scheme
